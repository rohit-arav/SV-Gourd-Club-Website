About Our Club

The Silicon Valley Gourd Club brings together artists and enthusiasts who share a passion for transforming gourds into beautiful works of art. Our monthly meetings provide opportunities to learn, share techniques, and connect with fellow artists.

We are proud to be a regional group of the California Gourd Society.

We meet on the 2nd Sunday of each month from 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM. Our meetings are held at members' homes or club houses in Campbell, providing a warm and creative environment for sharing our love of gourd art.

All of our monthly meetings include a workshop where members can learn new techniques and share their own tips.
